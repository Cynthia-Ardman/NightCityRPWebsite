# Missions Addendum: Actor Payment Tools and Payment Visibility

## 1. Remove Manual Player Payment From Fixer Mission Tools

On the Missions tab, under fixer tools, remove any manual “Pay Players” option.

Players should be paid automatically by the mission auto-pay system after the mission has occurred.

**Expected behavior**

* Fixers should not manually pay assigned player characters from the mission page.
* Player payment should happen through the automated mission payment flow.
* Fixers/admins should still be able to view player payment status, success, or failure.

**Acceptance criteria**

* No manual Pay Players button appears in the fixer mission tools.
* Player payment is handled automatically.
* Fixers/admins can still see whether players were paid.
* Failed automatic player payments are visible for staff follow-up.
* Manual actor payment remains available.

---

## 2. Pay Actors Search and Multi-Select

The **Pay Actors** tool should allow fixers/admins to search for actor names and select any number of actors to pay.

**Pay Actors form should include**

* Search field for actor/player name
* Multi-select actor results
* Payment amount field
* Confirmation button

**Behavior**

* Fixer searches by name.
* Matching users/actors appear in the selectable list.
* Fixer can select one actor or multiple actors.
* Fixer enters the payment amount.
* On submit, each selected actor is paid that amount.
* Payment records are tied to the mission.

**Acceptance criteria**

* Pay Actors supports name search.
* Pay Actors supports selecting multiple actors.
* Payment amount is required.
* Selected actors are paid through the configured payment flow.
* Actor payments are recorded against the mission.
* Actor payment notices are posted to `#npc-spending`.
* Actor payments are logged to `#banking` where appropriate.
* Duplicate actor payments for the same mission are prevented or clearly warned.

---

## 3. Actor Payment List / Payment History Tab

Add a tab or section in the mission detail view that shows actors paid for that mission and how much they were paid.

**Suggested location**

Inside the mission detail view, under fixer tools, add a tab or section called:

`Actor Payments`

**Actor Payments should show**

* Actor/user name
* Payment amount
* Payment status
* Payment timestamp
* Paid by / fixer who initiated payment
* Payment method/source, such as manual Pay Actors
* Any failed payment/error state

**Acceptance criteria**

* Fixers/admins can see a list of actors paid for the mission.
* Fixers/admins can see how much each actor was paid.
* Fixers/admins can see whether each payment succeeded or failed.
* Actor payment records update after using Pay Actors.
* Failed payments are visible and actionable.
* Regular players do not see staff-only payment management details unless intentionally allowed.

---

## 4. Payment Tracking Separation

Player payments and actor payments should remain separate.

### Player payments

* Automated only.
* Based on assigned players/characters.
* Visible to fixers/admins as payment status.
* Not manually initiated from the mission fixer tools.

### Actor payments

* Manual through Pay Actors.
* Searchable by actor/player name.
* Multi-select.
* Payment amount entered by fixer/admin.
* Visible in Actor Payments tab/section.

**Acceptance criteria**

* Player and actor payment records are stored separately or clearly typed.
* Player auto-pay does not interfere with actor payment.
* Actor payment does not affect player auto-pay.
* Mission status logic can correctly tell:

  * Players unpaid
  * Players paid but actors unpaid
  * Everyone paid
